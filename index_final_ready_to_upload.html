Our Story

Built on a Father's Dream

AR Digital Printing & Crafty Shop is more than a business — it is a dream that never gave up.

It all started with our Papa, who built this business from the ground up starting in Iligan City, then growing to General Santos City, reaching every barangay along the way.

"Even when life got hard, Papa never stopped believing in this business."

When our Mama was diagnosed with breast cancer, our family returned to Iligan and eventually settled here in Cagayan de Oro. What started as a home-based operation has now grown into a real, physical shop — a testament to resilience, love, and faith.

🏠 Started in Iligan City
📦 Expanded to General Santos City
💪 Returned to Iligan for family
🏪 Now open in Cagayan de Oro — with a real shop!
